# Noor Palette — Quiet Dawn + Sage

## Brand Colors
| Token | Hex | Role |
|-------|-----|------|
| brand-primary | #B8904A | Gold. Hero CTAs, brand mark, up-next highlight, active tab pill |
| brand-secondary | #4A5B6E | Slate. Subheaders, nav chrome, weight-bearing dividers |
| brand-tertiary | #7A9481 | Sage. Affirm actions ("Mark complete", "Log prayer"), Faith pillar, synced indicator |
| brand-accent | #D4A574 | Warm amber. Hover fills on gold buttons, "Up next" chip tint, warm emphasis |

## Surfaces & Structure
| Token | Hex | Role |
|-------|-----|------|
| bg | #FAF7F2 | Page canvas. Warm cream — never pure white at page level |
| surface | #FFFFFF | Cards, modals, inputs, dropdowns. "Paper on linen" contrast |
| border | #E8E3DB | All hairlines, dividers, card edges. Single border token |

## Text (Ink Scale)
| Token | Hex | Role |
|-------|-----|------|
| ink-primary | #1C1E22 | Headings, body copy, filled button labels |
| ink-secondary | #6B7280 | Descriptions, meta info, helper text, timestamps |
| ink-tertiary | #9CA0A8 | Placeholders, disabled states, muted captions |

## Sage Scale (Full Ramp)
| Token | Hex | Role |
|-------|-----|------|
| sage-50 | #EEF1EC | Calm-state card backgrounds, ghost button fills, chip bgs ("Synced", "On track") |
| sage-200 | #D4DDCF | Borders for sage-tinted cards, ghost sage button outlines |
| sage-500 | #7A9481 | Solid sage button fills, Faith pillar accent bar, completion icons |
| sage-800 | #3E5544 | Text on sage-50/200 backgrounds. Never use primary ink on sage fills |

| Success/synced/on-track | #EEF1EC | #3E5544 | #D4DDCF | — |
| Attention/up-next/pending | #F7ECD9 | #6B4E1E | #E8D4A8 | — |

| Error/destructive | #F7E8E4 | #8B3A2B | — | #B8553E |

## Decision Rules
1. **Gold vs. sage**: Gold = active/pending (Fajr coming, add task). Sage = settled/protected (prayer complete, synced). Default to sage if unclear.
2. **Pillars as accents, not themes**: Pillar colors tint within a page, don't own the background. Faith page is still cream + sage, not "green everywhere."
3. **Hierarchy through weight, not color**: Primary vs secondary button differ in fill weight (solid vs ghost), then color. Never pair solid gold + solid sage as peers.
